Welcome to poultry heaven

Welcome to Selsar Chicken Farm, where every feather tells a story of passion, perseverance, and farm-to-table excellence. Nestled amidst the rolling hills of Kiserian, our farm stands as a testament to the dreams of two individuals: Oundo and Elizabeth. In 2010, fueled by a shared vision and a love for the land, they embarked on a journey to cultivate something truly special. With a modest flock of chickens and boundless determination, they transformed their dream into reality. Through years of hard work, innovation, and unwavering dedication, they grew their farm into a thriving hub of poultry production. From the fertile soil to the endless skies, every aspect of our operation reflects their commitment to sustainability, quality, and community. Today, Selsar Chicken Farm is more than just a business; it's a legacy. Our diverse range of chicken products – from succulent whole chickens to flavorful chicken sausages – is a testament to the passion and expertise that infuse every aspect of our farm. As we continue to grow and evolve, our roots remain firmly planted in the soil of Kiserian, where it all began. Join us on this extraordinary journey, and experience the difference that passion makes in every bite.
